Overview

*F.R.I.E.N.D.S* is a beloved American sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004. It follows the lives of six friends—Rachel, Ross, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe—living in New York City. Known for its humor, heart, and iconic catchphrases, the show remains a cultural phenomenon.

Key Info

  • Genre: Sitcom / Comedy
  • Seasons: 10
  • Episodes: 236
  • Original Air Dates: 1994–2004
  • Created by: David Crane & Marta Kauffman

Main Cast

  • Jennifer Aniston as Rachel Green
  • Courteney Cox as Monica Geller
  • Lisa Kudrow as Phoebe Buffay
  • Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani
  • Matthew Perry as Chandler Bing
  • David Schwimmer as Ross Geller

Characters

Rachel Green: A fashion enthusiast who starts as a waitress and works her way up in the industry. Monica Geller: The organized chef and Ross's sister. Phoebe Buffay: The quirky and lovable musician. Joey Tribbiani: A charming actor with a big appetite and bigger heart. Chandler Bing: Known for his sarcasm and mysterious job in data processing. Ross Geller: A paleontologist with a complicated romantic history.

Reviews

*F.R.I.E.N.D.S* received critical and commercial success, earning numerous awards including an Emmy for Outstanding Comedy Series. Its relatable themes, humor, and character dynamics have made it one of the most watched and re-watched TV shows of all time.
